Traditional Formulations

Classical Ayurvedic Preparations

Authentic herbal medicines prepared using time-honored methods

Churna (Powders)

Fine herbal powders made from dried roots, leaves, and fruits. Easy to consume with warm water, honey, or ghee. Examples: Triphala Churna, Ashwagandha Churna, Hingwashtak Churna.

Kashayam (Decoctions)

Concentrated herbal extracts prepared by boiling herbs in water. Highly potent and fast-acting. Examples: Dasamoola Kashayam, Punarnavadi Kashayam.

Avaleha (Jams)

Sweet herbal preparations made with jaggery or sugar. Palatable and nourishing. Examples: Chyawanprash, Brahma Rasayana, Ashwagandha Avaleha.

Tailam (Oils)

Medicated oils prepared by infusing herbs in base oils. Used for massage and external application. Examples: Brahmi Tailam, Dhanwantaram Tailam, Ksheerabala Tailam.

Vati (Tablets)

Compressed herbal tablets for convenient consumption. Standardized dosages for consistent results. Examples: Chandraprabha Vati, Arogyavardhini Vati.

Arishtam (Fermented)

Herbal wines prepared through natural fermentation. Enhanced bioavailability and therapeutic action. Examples: Ashokarishtam, Dasamoolarishtam.
